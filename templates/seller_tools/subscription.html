Frequently Asked Questions
Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

What happens if I exceed my listing limit?

You'll need to upgrade to add more listings, or wait for existing listings to sell or expire.

How do commission rates work?

Commission is deducted from your sale price when a buyer completes a purchase. Higher tiers get lower commission rates.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Cancel anytime. Your subscription benefits continue until the end of your billing period.
