Seller Guide

Everything you need to know to sell successfully on HeroesAndMore

Getting Started

1. Create Your Account

Sign up for free and complete your profile. A complete profile with a photo helps build trust with buyers.

2. Create Your First Listing

Click "Sell" in the navigation bar. Add clear photos, a detailed description, set your price, and choose your shipping options.

3. Connect Payment

Link your Stripe account to receive payments. This is required before you can receive funds from sales.

Tips for Great Listings

  • Use high-quality photos - Include multiple angles and close-ups of any flaws
  • Be accurate about condition - Honestly describe any wear, damage, or defects
  • Include grading info - If your item is PSA/BGS/CGC graded, include the cert number
  • Price competitively - Research similar items to price appropriately
  • Write detailed descriptions - Include year, set, player/character, and any special features
  • Respond quickly - Answer buyer questions promptly to close sales faster

Fees & Payments

Listing Fee Free
Transaction Fee 3% of sale price
Payment Processing ~2.9% + $0.30 (Stripe)
Payout Time 3-5 business days after buyer confirms receipt
