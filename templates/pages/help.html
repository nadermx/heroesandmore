Help Center

Find answers to common questions

Buying

Simply find an item you like, click "Buy Now" for fixed-price items or place a bid on auctions. You'll be guided through our secure checkout process powered by Stripe.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure Stripe payment system.

All purchases are covered by our buyer protection. If an item doesn't match the description or doesn't arrive, you're eligible for a full refund.

Selling

Click "Sell" in the navigation bar, fill out the listing form with photos and details, set your price, and publish. It's free to list!

Listing is free! We only charge a $0.30 transaction fee plus a commission (based on your seller tier) when your item sells.

Funds are released to your connected Stripe account once the buyer confirms receipt of the item, typically within 3-5 business days after delivery.

Account

Click "Get Started" and sign up with your email or Google account. It's free and only takes a few seconds.

Verified seller status is granted to sellers who maintain a high rating and complete identity verification. Contact support for more details.
