Safety Tips

Stay safe while buying and selling collectibles

For Buyers

Always use our checkout

Never pay outside of HeroesAndMore. Our secure checkout protects your purchase with buyer protection.

Check seller ratings

Review the seller's feedback and ratings before purchasing. Verified sellers have passed our verification process.

Ask questions

If something isn't clear, message the seller. A legitimate seller will be happy to answer questions and provide additional photos.

Verify graded items

For PSA, BGS, or CGC items, verify the certification number on the grading company's website before purchasing.

For Sellers

Ship with tracking

Always use a shipping service with tracking and consider insurance for valuable items.

Document everything

Take photos of items before shipping and keep shipping receipts as proof of shipment.

Be accurate

Accurately describe item condition. Misrepresenting items leads to returns and negative reviews.

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Requests to pay outside of HeroesAndMore
  • Prices that seem too good to be true
  • Sellers who won't answer questions or provide additional photos
  • Pressure to complete a transaction quickly
  • Requests for personal information beyond what's needed for shipping
Buyer Protection

All purchases through our checkout are protected.

Report an Issue

If you encounter suspicious activity, let us know.
